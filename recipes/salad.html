Salad

What is a salad?

A salad is a dish consisting of a mixture of small pieces of food, usually vegetables or fruit. It is often served cold, typically as an appetizer or side dish.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
