Cake

What is a cake?

A cake is a sweet baked dessert, typically made from flour, sugar, eggs, and butter. It can be flavored with various ingredients such as chocolate, fruit, or nuts, and is often decorated with icing or frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
